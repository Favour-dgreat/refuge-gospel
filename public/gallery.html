Community Outreaches

We engage in annual outreach to rural communities in various parts of Nigeria. Some of our recently visited communities includes: Nihi Etche, Akwa Etche, Odagwa Etche, Odufor Etche, Afara Etche, Akwa Etche, Bodo City-Gokana (all in Rivers State, Nigeria), and communities in Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria, to mention a few, fulfilling our rescue mandate as we move into these communites with the gospel of salvation and basic neccessities.
