WHO WE ARE

Refuge Gospel International Church is a church of all races, colour, and class. The Church is involved in global discipleship, mission Work and charitable deeds aimed at supporting humanity and Peace for Kingdom growth; this is our divine commission and mandate. A Church with a burden to give man whole solace of Spirit, Soul and Body; to serve God and His people faithfully in His Will.

Our Mission

We are called to rescue and to raise a living, glorious, triumphant people with a Refuge Gospel Mission, giving whole Solace of Spirit, Soul and Body to our world of outreach.

OUR TENETS OF FAITH

The Trinity

We believe in the Unity of the Father, Son and the HolySpirit, in the Godhead.
(i.e we believe in the eternal God Head of the Father, Son and HolySpirit (Trinity)
[1st John 5:7]).

The Holy Scripture

We believe in the divine inspiration and infallibility of the Holy Scriptures as originally given and its Supreme authority in all matters of faith and conduct (we stand for the whole Bible as the whole revelation of God in Christ, and that what God revealed in Christ is every believer's heritage). 2nd Timothy 3:16
